Nth roots of unity

Nth roots of unity are the values that satisfy the following.

$$ z^n=1 $$

In polar representation, the roots can be expressed as follows.

$$ z=e^{2\pi i/n} $$

In[]:
import sympy
from sympy import solve, symbols, arg, simplify

z = symbols("z")
for i in range(1,10):
    res = solve(z ** i - 1, z)
    print(f"{i}\t{res}")

Let's plot the 6th roots of unity. $$ z^6=1\\ z=e^{2\pi i/6} $$

In[]:
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
from sympy import re, im

r=1
fig = plt.figure(figsize=(8, 8))
ax = plt.subplot(projection='polar')
res = solve(z ** 6 - 1, z)
for p in res:
    theta=arg(p)
    ax.plot(theta,r,marker='o',markersize=4, color="black") 
    ax.plot((0, theta), (0, r),'--',color="lightgray") 
    ax.text(theta-0.07, r, f'P({re(p)},{im(p)})',fontsize=16)
